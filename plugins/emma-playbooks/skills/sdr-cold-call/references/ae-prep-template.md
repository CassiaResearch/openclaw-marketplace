# AE Prep Briefing (Slack)

Send a structured Slack message to the assigned AE before their demo. This ensures no blind spots and no scrambling.

## Structure

The briefing should include:
1. **Who:** Prospect name, title, company, industry
2. **Pain points:** What they're struggling with (from discovery)
3. **Context:** How they heard about us, current tools/solutions, team size
4. **What to emphasize:** Which CoPilot AI capabilities map to their needs
5. **Likely questions/objections:** Based on what came up in discovery
6. **Decision maker info:** Who makes the final call, any other stakeholders
7. **Timeline:** Why now? Any urgency signals?

## Example

> @[AE Name] — prep for your demo with [Prospect Name]:
>
> • **Who:** [Name], [Title] at [Company] ([Industry], [Size])
> • **Pain:** [Specific pain point 1], [Specific pain point 2]
> • **Context:** Currently using [current tool], found us via [source]
> • **Emphasize:** [Relevant CPAI capability], [Another capability]
> • **Watch for:** They asked about [pricing/compliance/feature] — be ready
> • **DM:** [Name] is the decision maker, [other stakeholder] also involved
> • **Timeline:** Looking to decide within [timeframe]

## Rules

- Send via Slack DM to the AE (or in the relevant deal channel if one exists)
- Send within 1 hour of booking the demo
- Be specific — generic briefings waste the AE's time
- Include the HubSpot contact link so the AE can review the full record
