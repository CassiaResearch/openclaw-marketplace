# SDR → AE Handoff Email Template

The handoff email connects the prospect to their AE. It should be warm, personalized, and bridge the relationship.

## Structure

- Address the prospect by name
- Reference something specific from the discovery call (pain point, goal, or context)
- Introduce the AE by name and role
- Set expectation for what the demo will cover
- Keep it concise — 3-5 sentences max

## Example

> Hi [Prospect Name],
>
> It was great chatting with you today! I'd love to introduce you to [AE Name], who will be your point of contact going forward. [He/She]'ll dive deeper into [pain point 1] and [pain point 2] during your demo.
>
> [AE Name] has helped dozens of [industry] teams solve similar challenges and I know you'll be in great hands.
>
> Looking forward to hearing how it goes!
>
> Best,
> Emma

## Rules

- Always CC the AE on the email
- Send within 30 minutes of the call
- Reference specific pain points from the call — do NOT use generic language
- The AE should reply to this email introducing themselves (per Data Hygiene Handbook)
